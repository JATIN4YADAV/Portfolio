Real Life Problem

The Decline of Outdoor Play

I have observed with concern the shift in children's activities from outdoor play to digital screen time. In my own childhood, evenings were filled with friends gathering to play cricket and other games. Today, that scene is rarely seen, as children are often engrossed in social media, gaming, and online content. This trend affects physical health, social development, and creativity. I believe it's important to encourage activities that foster face-to-face interaction and a deeper connection with the physical world, which are crucial for a well-rounded upbringing.

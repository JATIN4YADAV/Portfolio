Social Impact

The Problem

I have observed with concern the shift in children's activities from outdoor play to digital screen time. In my own childhood, evenings were filled with friends gathering to play cricket and other games. Today, that scene is rarely seen, as children are often engrossed in social media, gaming, and online content. This trend affects physical health, social development, and creativity. I believe it's important to encourage activities that foster face-to-face interaction and a deeper connection with the physical world, which are crucial for a well-rounded upbringing.

The Solution

I believe the very tools that contribute to the problem can also be part of the solution. I'm interested in developing a mobile application or a web platform that encourages outdoor activities through gamification. This could include features like geo-located challenges to explore local parks and landmarks, team-based leaderboards to foster friendly competition, and digital badges and rewards for completing physical activities. By merging the appeal of technology with the benefits of outdoor play, we can create a hybrid experience that motivates kids to stay active and connected with their community, proving that technology can be used to enrich our lives in tangible ways.
